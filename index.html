Book preferences:

I generally like all of the stuff I read. It's hard for me to hate a particular book or genre. I can appreciate the writing skill involved in any work even if it doesn't match my tastes. However, here are some trends I have identified in my preferences:

  • Favourite genre: New Wave science fiction
  • Detailed worldbuilding, with attention to scientific accuracy and historical precedent
  • Intercultural contact, revealing how each side reacts to the unfamiliar
  • Pushes the boundaries of the human experience -- involves a greater extent of pain and joy than in real life
  • Slice of life, mono no aware
  • Complicated characters and plots
  • Ambivalent emotions e.g. nostalgia
  • Philosophical. Ideas that challenge my own worldview and can be assimilated. In reading, one goal in addition to entertainment is personal growth.
  • Beautiful, poetic, "Borgesian" quotes
  • I don't mind spoilers much since I value the story's journey, not just the ending.
