Preferences:

I generally like all of the stuff I read, whether that's due to my careful selection, appreciation for writing skill, or simply because I find negativity exhausting. However, here are some trends I have identified in my taste:

  • Favourite genre: New Wave science fiction
  • Detailed worldbuilding, with attention to scientific accuracy and historical precedent
  • Intercultural contact, revealing how each side reacts to the unfamiliar
  • Pushing the boundaries of the human experience, a greater extent of pain and joy than in real life, yet still fundamentally relatable
  • Slice of life, mono no aware
  • Complicated characters and plots
  • Ambivalent emotions e.g. nostalgia
  • Philosophical themes, ideas that challenge my own worldview and can be assimilated. When reading, one of my goals besides entertainment is personal growth.
  • Beautiful quotes. Think Borges or Dune.
  • I don't mind spoilers much since I value the story's journey, not just the ending.
